Get to know mars Robotics

MARSRobotics brings to you recognized world class expertise in Unmanned Autonomous Aerial Vehicles (Drones) by a team of dedicated goal oriented engineers and administrators to meet your needs and expectation of world class services in surveying, aerial photography, inspection, precision farming, surveillance, homeland security, and the like. Founded by Dr. Mohammad Amin Al Jarrah, a Stanford University Graduate of Aeronautics and Astronautics in 1989 with more than 80 refereed publications related to Drones Technology since 1988 with the first UAV project known as JUST UAV project launched in 1992 in corporation with the Jordanian Armed Forces.

At Mars Robotics, innovation is at the core of everything we do. Our team continually pushes the boundaries of drone technology, integrating cutting-edge advancements in artificial intelligence, machine learning, and real-time data processing to deliver solutions that exceed industry standards. Our commitment to excellence ensures that every project we undertake is executed with precision, reliability, and a customer-centric approach. Whether you're seeking advanced aerial mapping or robust security surveillance, we bring unmatched expertise to the table.
