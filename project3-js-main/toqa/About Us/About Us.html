Our Services


Surveying and Mapping

using (UAVs) equipped with cameras and sensors to capture high-resolution images and data for creating accurate maps, 3D models, and detailed surveys of land or infrastructure.

Aerial Filming and Photography

drones equipped with cameras to capture stunning high-quality images and videos from unique perspectives, often used in cinematography, real estate, and marketing.

Agriculture

involves using drones equipped with sensors and cameras to monitor crop health, assess soil conditions, and optimize irrigation and fertilization for more efficient and sustainable farming practices.

Solar Cells Inspection using MARSRobotics UAV

Enhance the efficiency and reliability of your solar energy systems with Mars Robotics UAV inspection services.

LiDAR

Mars Robotics offers state-of-the-art LiDAR mapping services for precise 3D data acquisition. Our UAVs equipped with advanced LiDAR technology deliver accurate mapping of terrains and structures.

Mining

Mining drones are used to capture aerial data for mapping, surveying, and monitoring mining sites, improving efficiency, safety, and resource management in the industry.
